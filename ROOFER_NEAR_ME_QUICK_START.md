## ⚠️ CRITICAL MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. ❌ **Buying fake reviews** (Google will ban you)
2. ❌ **Inconsistent NAP** (use exact format everywhere)
3. ❌ **Not responding to reviews** (kills trust)
4. ❌ **Keyword stuffing** ("best roofer best roofing...")
5. ❌ **Creating multiple GBPs** (without physical presence)
6. ❌ **Ignoring negative reviews** (respond professionally)
7. ❌ **Not uploading photos** (reduces GBP visibility by 50%)
