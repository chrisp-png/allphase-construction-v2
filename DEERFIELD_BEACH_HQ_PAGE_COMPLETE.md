## 21-Point Inspection Overview (NEW)

### Exterior Assessment (7 Points)
1. Roofing material condition, remaining lifespan, and weathering patterns
2. Missing, damaged, cracked, or loose shingles, tiles, or membrane sections
3. Flashing integrity at chimneys, vents, walls, valleys, and eaves
4. Valley condition, debris accumulation, and water channeling effectiveness
5. Ridge cap installation, ventilation adequacy, and wind damage indicators
6. Soffit, fascia, and trim condition including rot, pest damage, or separation
7. Gutter and downspout functionality, proper drainage, and water management

### Structural Evaluation (4 Points)
8. Roof decking condition, structural soundness, and deterioration
9. Rafter and truss integrity including deflection, cracking, or movement
10. Load-bearing capacity and structural concerns
11. Roof-to-wall connections and HVHZ compliance (where applicable)

### Interior Inspection (5 Points)
12. Attic ventilation adequacy, airflow patterns, and temperature control
13. Insulation condition, R-value compliance, and coverage uniformity
14. Active or historical water intrusion evidence including staining patterns
15. Moisture readings, humidity levels, and condensation concerns
16. Mold, mildew, fungal growth, or biological contamination

### Penetrations & Accessories (5 Points)
17. Chimney flashing, counter-flashing, and cap condition
18. Plumbing vent pipe boots, seals, and collar integrity
19. Skylight condition, flashing, and seal effectiveness
20. HVAC penetrations, equipment supports, and curb flashing
21. Satellite dishes, antennas, and auxiliary equipment mounting
