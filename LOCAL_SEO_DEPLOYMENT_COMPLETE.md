## 📋 Changes Applied to Each Page

### 1. LocalBusiness Schema
Every city page now has a complete LocalBusiness schema with:
- **@type:** "RoofingContractor"
- **Name:** "All Phase Construction USA"
- **City-specific coordinates:** Latitude/longitude for precise local ranking
- **Service radius:** 15 miles from each city
- **Aggregate rating:** 4.9 stars with 150 reviews
- **Services offered:** Roof repair, replacement, inspection, commercial, emergency
- **Credentials:** HVHZ certification, dual licensing
- **Contact info:** (754) 227-5605
- **Business hours:** Monday-Friday 7AM-6PM, Saturday 8AM-4PM
- **Payment methods:** Cash, credit card, check, financing
- **Social profiles:** Facebook, Yelp, Google Business

### 2. Breadcrumb Schema
Three-level breadcrumb navigation for every city:
```
Home > Service Areas > {City Name}
```

### 3. Optimized Meta Titles
**New Format:** `{City} Roofer | HVHZ Certified | All Phase`

**Examples:**
- Fort Lauderdale Roofer | HVHZ Certified | All Phase
- Boca Raton Roofer | HVHZ Certified | All Phase
- Wellington Roofer | HVHZ Certified | All Phase

**Benefits:**
- Targets "roofer" + city keywords
- Highlights HVHZ certification (competitive advantage)
- Brand recognition with "All Phase"
- ~55 characters (optimal for SERPs)

### 4. Optimized Meta Descriptions
**New Format:** `{City} FL roofing contractor. HVHZ certified. Tile, metal, shingle systems. Free inspections. (754) 227-5605`

**Benefits:**
- City + state targeting
- Key certification (HVHZ)
- Service variety (tile, metal, shingle)
- Call-to-action (free inspections)
- Phone number for immediate contact
- ~150 characters (optimal length)
