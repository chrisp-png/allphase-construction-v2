## 🎯 CRITICAL OFF-SITE ACTIONS (YOU MUST DO)

### Priority 1: Google Business Profile (GBP)
**This is 70% of local SEO success!**

#### Setup Options:
1. **Service Area Business (SAB)** - Single profile
   - Use your main office: 1280 SW 36th Ave Unit 106, Pompano Beach
   - List all service areas (Broward & Palm Beach counties)
   - Hide address (since you go to customer locations)
   - **Pros**: Easier to manage, one profile to optimize
   - **Cons**: May not rank as well in distant cities

2. **Multiple Locations** (Advanced)
   - Create separate GBP for major cities IF you have physical presence
   - Each needs unique address, phone, or staffing
   - **Pros**: Better local rankings in each city
   - **Cons**: Harder to manage, requires legitimate local presence

#### GBP Optimization Checklist:
- [ ] Complete 100% of profile fields
- [ ] Add primary category: "Roofing Contractor"
- [ ] Add secondary categories: "Roof Repair Service", "Construction Company"
- [ ] Upload 50+ high-quality photos (exterior, team, before/after)
- [ ] Add detailed business description (750 characters max)
- [ ] List all services with descriptions
- [ ] Set accurate hours (including emergency availability)
- [ ] Enable messaging
- [ ] Add service areas (all cities you serve)
- [ ] Create Google Posts weekly (updates, tips, completed projects)
- [ ] Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours

#### Review Strategy (CRITICAL):
**Goal: 50+ reviews in next 3 months**

How to get reviews:
1. **After Every Job**: Text/email review request link within 24 hours
2. **Incentivize**: Offer $25 gift card for honest reviews
3. **Make it Easy**: Send direct Google review link
4. **Timing**: Ask when customer is happiest (after job completion)
5. **Response**: Reply to every review (positive and negative)

Review request template:
```
Hi [Name]! We're so glad we could help with your roof. Would you mind sharing your experience? It helps local homeowners find quality roofers.

Leave a review: [Google Review Link]

Thanks!
- All Phase Construction USA
```

#### NAP Consistency (CRITICAL):
Use **EXACTLY** this format everywhere:

```
All Phase Construction USA
1280 SW 36th Ave Unit 106
Pompano Beach, FL 33069
(754) 227-5605
```

**Do NOT vary**:
- "Suite" vs "Unit"
- "Avenue" vs "Ave"
- Phone format: (754) 227-5605 NOT 754-227-5605

## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Inconsistent NAP**: Different addresses/phones hurt rankings
2. **Ignoring Reviews**: Must respond to all reviews
3. **Keyword Stuffing**: GBP description should sound natural
4. **Fake Reviews**: Google detects and penalizes this
5. **Wrong Categories**: "Roofing Contractor" should be primary
6. **No Photos**: GBP with photos get 42% more direction requests
7. **Duplicate GBP Listings**: Confuses Google, hurts rankings
