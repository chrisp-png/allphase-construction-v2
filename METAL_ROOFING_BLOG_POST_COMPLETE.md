## Content Overview

This is an in-depth educational guide (3,500+ words) that differentiates All Phase from competitors by exposing industry shortcuts and explaining technical details most homeowners never hear about.

### Key Topics Covered

1. **Gauge Selection (24 vs. 26-gauge)**
   - Why 24-gauge is the quality standard
   - Fastener pull-through risk with thinner metal
   - Cost vs. performance trade-offs
   - HVHZ wind rating considerations

2. **Exposed Fastener System Problems**
   - Puddle washer deterioration from UV exposure
   - Thermal expansion causing fastener back-out
   - Lack of secondary water barrier
   - Hidden warranty requirements (annual inspections)
   - Long-term cost of "budget-friendly" choice

3. **Aluminum vs. Steel for Coastal Areas**
   - One-mile coastal rule of thumb
   - Salt air corrosion issues with steel
   - Galvalume coating limitations
   - Premium coating costs vs. aluminum
   - Supply chain and pricing considerations

4. **Standing Seam vs. Snap Lock Systems**
   - Mechanically seamed (field-locked) explained
   - Snap lock sealant requirements (2 continuous beads)
   - Common contractor shortcut: partial sealant application
   - How unsealed sections fail in hurricanes
   - Why proper snap lock costs same as standing seam

5. **Clip Spacing and Wind Ratings**
   - How clip spacing determines uplift resistance
   - Variable ratings with same panels (110 mph to 200+ mph)
   - Eave/ridge zones vs. field of roof
   - Contractor corner-cutting tactics
   - Building inspector limitations (5-minute visits)

6. **Photographic Documentation**
   - Why photos protect homeowners
   - Insurance claim denial risk without proof
   - What should be documented (clips, fasteners, underlayment)
   - Questions to ask contractors
   - Red flags when contractors refuse

7. **Common Myths Debunked**
   - "Metal roofs are noisy" — residential vs. barn construction
   - "Metal roofs make houses hotter" — reflectivity vs. absorption
   - Proper assembly layers explained
   - Ventilation optimization (ridge vents, solar attic fans)

8. **Critical Questions to Ask**
   - Gauge specification and justification
   - Clip spacing at eaves, ridge, and field
   - System type (mechanically seamed vs. snap lock)
   - Sealant application details (if snap lock)
   - Secondary water barrier installation
   - Steel vs. aluminum recommendation for location
   - Photographic documentation commitment
   - Manufacturer specifications comparison
