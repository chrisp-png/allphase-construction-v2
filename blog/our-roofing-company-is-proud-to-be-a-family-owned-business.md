## What Dual Licensing Means for You

All Phase Construction carries two licenses that demonstrate our expertise and compliance with Florida's stringent roofing requirements:

- **General Contractor License (CGC-1526236)** - Authorizes us to manage construction projects of all sizes and complexities across multiple trades
- **Roofing Contractor License (CCC-1331464)** - Specialized roofing license issued by Florida's Department of Business and Professional Regulation, signifying proven expertise in roofing systems, techniques, and compliance

This dual licensing isn't just paperwork. It means:

- We meet Florida's highest standards for contractor conduct and competence
- We're authorized to work on residential and commercial projects without limitations
- We carry continuing education in roofing science and Florida Building Code compliance
- We maintain bonds and insurance to protect your investment

Many single-licensed contractors can't oversee complex projects or coordinate multiple trades. Our dual licensing gives you access to a full-service construction company that specializes in roofing.

## HVHZ Certification: Your Hurricane Protection

All Phase Construction is fully certified to work in HVHZ (High Velocity Hurricane Zone) areas. This certification confirms that we meet the most stringent building requirements in the nation.

HVHZ certification means:

- Our materials are Miami-Dade County approved for 180+ mph wind resistance
- Our installation methods follow guidelines designed to keep roofs intact during catastrophic hurricanes
- We understand pressure equalization, fastener spacing, and edge details that protect your home
- Insurance companies recognize our HVHZ-compliant work and may offer discounts
- Your roof has the best chance of surviving the next major hurricane

Whether your property is in Broward County or Palm Beach County, HVHZ standards apply to any significant roofing work. We're not just familiar with these requirements—we excel at them.
